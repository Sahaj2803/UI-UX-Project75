Introduction
⇒ Introduction to AI-Based Wildlife Conservation :-
Wildlife conservation is important to protect animals and nature. However, tracking and monitoring animals in forests, oceans, and remote areas can be very difficult. This is where Artificial Intelligence (AI) helps!
⇒ How AI Helps in Wildlife Conservation :-
AI uses smart technology to track, study, and protect wildlife more easily and accurately. Here’s how:
  • Cameras & AI: AI studies photos and videos from cameras in forests to identify animals and track their movements.
  • Sound Tracking: AI listens to animal sounds to find and study them in jungles and oceans.
  • Drones & Satellites: AI processes aerial images to monitor animals, forests, and environmental changes.
  • Predicting Problems: AI can predict threats like poaching, habitat destruction, or disease outbreaks in animals.
