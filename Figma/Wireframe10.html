Future Directions In AI Conservation
1. AI-Powered Drones for Wildlife Monitoring :-
  • Drones with AI cameras will track animals from the sky without disturbing them.
  • They will detect poachers, track endangered species, and monitor forests in real-time.
2. Smarter AI Cameras & Sensors :-
  • AI cameras will automatically identify animals and send alerts when needed.
  • Motion and heat sensors will track animals even in the dark or thick forests.
3. AI + DNA Tracking :-
  • AI will analyze DNA samples (like hair or droppings) to identify rare species and check their health.
  • This will help scientists find and protect animals without needing to catch them.
