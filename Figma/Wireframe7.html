AI Algorithm For Wildlife Monitoring
  1. Movement Tracking & GPS :-
⇒ AI studies GPS data from collars or tags placed on animals to track their movement and migration patterns.
⇒ It helps understand where animals go, where they find food, and how they adapt to climate change.
  1. Predicting Threats & Behavior :-
⇒ AI can predict dangers like poaching, natural disasters, or diseases affecting wildlife.
⇒ It helps conservationists take action before it’s too late.
