Implementation Case Studies
1. AI Saves Elephants from Poachers :-
  • 📍 Where? Africa
    🔍 Problem: Poachers were hunting elephants for ivory, and it was hard to track them in large forests.
    🤖 AI Solution:
    • AI-powered cameras and drones detected poachers and sent alerts to rangers.
    • AI analyzed elephant movements using GPS collars to track their migration.
      🎯 Result: More elephants were protected, and poaching reduced.
    🐘
    2. AI Helps Count Tigers :-
  • 📍 Where? India
    🔍 Problem: Tigers were difficult to count because they live in dense forests.
    🤖 AI Solution:
    • AI studied camera trap photos and recognized tigers by their unique stripe patterns.
    • It counted tigers automatically, making the process faster and more accurate.
      🎯 Result: Scientists could track tiger populations better and protect them.
